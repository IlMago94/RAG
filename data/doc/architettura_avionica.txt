Distributed avionics architecture

The avionics platform is composed of:
- 5 GNC/AOCS boards (Guidance, Navigation & Control / Attitude & Orbit Control System)
- 5 DACT boards (Data Acquisition & Control)
- 5 DPOD boards (Deployment & Payload Operations)
- 10 PAYME boards (Payload Management & Experiment)
- 5 PMU
- 4 EPS 
- 6 PDHT

The boards are interconnected via CAN bus and use the CSP protocol for communication.
Each board has a CSP identifier and can send/receive messages from other nodes.

the libcsp version will be 1.4

Connection example:
GNC/AOCS boards send attitude data to DACT boards, which process it and forward it to DPOD and PAYME boards. All boards can exchange CSP messages over CAN, ensuring a robust and distributed network.
