LibCSP (CubeSat Space Protocol) is a communication stack designed for embedded systems and satellites, especially CubeSats and small spacecraft.

Main features:
- Lightweight protocol, suitable for microcontrollers.
- Supports CAN bus, UART, I2C, and TCP/IP.
- Maximum supported number of nodes: 30.
- Used for communication between avionics subsystems.
- Provides routing, security, and packet handling.

Note: The 30-node limit is intended to keep the network efficient and simple to manage in embedded environments.
